What Drives Me?

6 resons why I do what I do day after day

Passion

A compelling feeling that everything I do touches the life of some other person, and a deep rooted desire to making this other person's life a better one.

Curiosity

An active mind and an open heart provide the opportunity to seeing new worlds and possibilities and, hence, bringing excitement and adventure to life.

Collaboration

Knowing that no success can be achieved in solitude, it brings joy working around other people and getting the opportunity to learn and unlearn as we progress together.

Before we begin, here are some rules to keep in mind:

  • No SPAM. I hate it the most.
  • No ads, special offers, or free gifts. I do not play online games, so there is no way I can win the lottery.
  • No politics, religion, or sex. I can’t care less.
  • No Promises made. I have the right not to reply, regardless of your noble cause.
  • No that’s not all. I reserve the right to change the rules the way I see fit and anytime.

Having said that, please go straight to the point in what you say. Just imagine how hard it is to keep up with the daily routine, and the amount of emails I currently process. The shorter your message is, the better, and the more attention it will get.
